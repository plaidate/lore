# Shrew

Pip, a shrew knight, must eat half her body weight — 12 grams — by
dawn, and the Mole Tyrant hoards the Grub Cellar under the eastern
mountains. Every bug you defeat is dinner: battles pay grams
alongside XP and seeds (the coin; the shop prices read in `g` —
one seed-gram). Classic Dragon Quest grammar throughout: grid
steps, bracketed dialogue, menu battles in a front-view scene.

The quest: hear out Elder Bram in VOLEHOLM, buy a Thorn Pin (Use
it from the Items menu to don it), fetch the ferrier's lost oar
from a chest in the western ferns, take her skiff (walk right onto
the river), fatten up on the marsh's midges and ticks, cross east,
and clear the two-floor Burrow — the Brass Key on floor 1 opens
floor 2's door, and the Tyrant waits past it. Beating him spills
the cellar: the feast fills the dawn meter and the sun comes up.

Controls: d-pad steps, A talks/opens/loots, B opens the pause menu
(Items / Status / Save; crank scrolls). The inn costs 5 seeds. If
the night wins, you wake at your last save with half your seeds.

Tips: Squeak at LV1, Nibble (drains) at LV2, Curl (guard) at LV3,
Mend at LV4 — the Tyrant wants LV4-5, a Curl opener, and Mend
underneath. Seed Cakes from Burrow chests are big battle heals.

The smoke autopilot beats the entire quest headlessly: shop, oar,
boat, marsh grind to LV4, both floors, boss, sunrise.
